# Taxed HQ Go-To-Market and Pricing Tests

## Launch Objective
Acquire first paying cohort from W-2 young professionals while validating conversion from interactive free tools to paid learning/workspace features.

## Offer Packaging

### Free Tier
- Access to baseline simulator and raise-vs-bonus simulator.
- Up to 2 saved projects.
- Single comparison per project.
- Basic explanations only.

### Pro Tier (Primary Paid Offer)
- Unlimited projects and comparisons.
- Full 3-module proficiency path.
- Advanced explanation cards and confidence tracking.
- Priority feature access.

### Premium Tier (Optional Add-On)
- Monthly live workshop/Q&A.
- Quarterly tax refresh labs.
- Downloadable playbooks/checklists.

## Initial Pricing Test Matrix (First 6 Weeks)

### Test A (Value-first monthly)
- Pro: $15/month
- Premium add-on: +$25/month

### Test B (Higher anchor with annual push)
- Pro: $19/month or $149/year
- Premium add-on: +$29/month

### Test C (One-time educational pass option)
- Pro pass: $89 one-time (12-month access)
- Premium add-on: +$39/month

## Experiment Design
- Run pricing tests on randomized cohorts by signup date window.
- Keep feature set constant; vary only package/pricing framing.
- Use same onboarding and acquisition channels during test windows.

## Acquisition Plan (First 90 Days)

### Channel 1: Short-form educational content
- Weekly micro-lessons on raise, bonus, and withholding misconceptions.
- CTA to free simulator with \"build your first scenario\" hook.

### Channel 2: Career-community partnerships
- Partner with early-career communities (Slack/Discord/newsletters).
- Offer free workshop + personalized scenario challenge.

### Channel 3: Referral loop
- Give users a \"compare your raise\" invite link.
- Reward both inviter and invitee with additional project slots/free trial days.

## Conversion Funnel Targets
- Visitor -> signup: 6-10%
- Signup -> first project: >= 60%
- First project -> first comparison: >= 40%
- First comparison -> paid conversion: 8-15% (early target)

## Messaging Framework
- **Primary headline:** \"Stop guessing. Model your taxes before you decide.\"
- **Pain-led angle:** \"Know your real take-home before accepting a raise, bonus, or new job.\"
- **Trust statement:** Educational clarity platform, not tax filing advice.

## Retention Plan
- Weekly challenge prompts (1 practical project/week).
- Monthly \"tax confidence score\" update email.
- New scenario templates released biweekly.

## KPI Dashboard (Weekly Review)
- Activation rate (first project created)
- Comparison adoption rate
- Free-to-paid conversion rate by test variant
- 4-week retention by plan
- Average time-to-first-insight
- Revenue per activated user

## Decision Rules After Test Window
- Select package with highest 4-week LTV proxy, not just day-1 conversion.
- Keep one low-friction entry product to reduce CAC pressure.
- Drop underperforming tiers/features that increase complexity without conversion lift.
